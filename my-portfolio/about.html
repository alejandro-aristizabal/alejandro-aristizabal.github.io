About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer with over 6 years of experience specializing in MLOps, technical leadership, and the end-to-end lifecycle of machine learning solutions. Currently, I lead innovative AI projects at Capgemini Engineering, where my work has contributed to significant revenue growth and operational efficiency.

With a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Universidad de los Andes and a strong foundation in process engineering from Eafit University, I bring a blend of technical expertise and strategic insight. My top skills include Azure DevOps, DevOps, and Microsoft Azure, and I am certified in areas such as High Concurrency Architecture, Encoder-Decoder Architecture, and Databricks Lakehouse Fundamentals.

I am passionate about mentoring and setting technical standards, developing scalable machine learning pipelines, and fostering collaborative team environments. My career is driven by a commitment to creating data-driven solutions that empower organizations to achieve measurable business impact.
